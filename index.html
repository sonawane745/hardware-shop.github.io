About Us
Who We Are

Formed in the year 1988, our business philosophy has been to continuously update and consolidate in the area of IT services always adopting a high level of integrity and professionalism.

The team at Comptek comprising professionals from the IT industry has a combined experience of over 75 years in various fields including computational hardware, network products and services as well as system software services delivering consultancy and design, operations support, software implementation.

Over the years, following the stated mission Comptek has endeavored to expand its range of products and services by developing new business relationships and garnering technical skills with the express objective of providing the customer with the choice of the best solutions in computing and networking.

Our commitment to quality and endeavor for satisfying clients has resulted in our developing a technical service team and standard operating procedure in every area of our business.

Our Mission

To be a leading provider of a comprehensive range of professional and high-quality services delivering consulting, design, implementation, commissioning and post installation support for products from world leading information technology providers.

Comptek’s Client Services

In an era when focuses on core business and core competencies are the new mantras in every organization, Comptek has structured a series of services, designed to help organizations to cater to this new theme. These services are designed to complement the leading edge technology we provide and allow you the customer to focus on being the best in your business. We call it “Customer Driven Engineering” and it transcends our desire to provide you with solutions that take the ‘work’ out of your business.
